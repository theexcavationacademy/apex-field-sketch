Sample Symbols

● Asbestos Sample Location — IDs S01, S02… (a-c = 3 samples per spot; vermiculite is sampled as this type)
▲ Lead Sample Location — IDs L01, L02…
◆ Other Contaminant Sample — IDs X01, X02… (mould, silica, mercury, PCBs, UFFI, refrigerants, radioactive, meth residue, guano, radon, arsenic)

Result Colours

●▲ Grey — awaiting lab results (working view only; prints black)
●▲ Black — tested, nothing found
● Red — asbestos positive
▲ Green — lead positive
● Orange — vermiculite positive (label becomes V## — its own thing)
◆ Purple ◆ — other contaminant positive (label becomes ?## — placeholder until the full symbol list is set)

Map Markings

▬ Thick black line — wall
▯ Gap in a wall — door / opening
═ Double parallel lines — hallway / corridor (one grid square wide)
≣ Rungs between rails — stairs
📷 Camera in a room's corner — tap it to photograph that room
1 Underlined number — room / location (details in Locations list)
SiO₂ Orange tag — silica / dust contamination noted in that room
✎ Blue pencil — this room has written notes (see Notes tab; prints on PDF page 2)
📷 Camera tag — this room has photos attached (view in Notes tab; print on PDF photo pages)

Substrate Codes

DW — drywall
DJC — drywall joint compound
DSC — drywall seam compound
TC/DW — textured coat on drywall
CT — ceramic tile
VSF — vinyl sheet flooring
W.P. VSF — wood-pattern vinyl sheet flooring
Plaster / Lathe — plaster on wood lathe
W / C / F — wall / ceiling / floor
S## a-c — asbestos sample set (3 per spot, compliance)
L## — lead paint-chip sample
V## — sample confirmed as vermiculite (was S##)
X## — other-contaminant sample (contaminant chosen when stamped)
?## — sample reclassified as a different contaminant than its original type

The exported drawing prints its own legend automatically — it only shows the positive-result lines for contaminants actually found on that job.
